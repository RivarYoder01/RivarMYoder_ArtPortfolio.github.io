Letters Received

Trayton Lowborn used to spend his time looking off the side of his room’s balcony. Part of him was admiring the view, part of him deep down hoped to see his fiancé return home. He doesn’t know why they left. Even though it had been four years Trayton still sends letters their way through Adalia, an arc-light phoenix that he had owned since he was young. On one night in particular he finally receives a short reply. Though he was happy they were okay, the lack of clarification was bitter-sweet.
