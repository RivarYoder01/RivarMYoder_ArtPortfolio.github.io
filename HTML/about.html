The Artist: Rivar

Growing up in North Dakota, Rivar found a deep and long term passion for drawing. Small doodles in note margins, drawings made through Microsoft Paint, or referencing a little wooden figure for poses, they always found time for this wide new world. Though they enjoy experimenting, they have always veered in the realm of digital art and media. Rivar is proficient in digital programs such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Figma, and Krita. With the rise of Artificial Intelligence, they strive to maintain originality and creativity- separate from any generative ‘work’. Creative storytelling and design have always been an old love they will continue to come back to.

Rivar attended Wayne State College in Wayne, Nebraska from the years 2022 to 2026. While striving to find a career that allows for that creative side to spin, their technical side began to rise. Computer Information Systems: Web Analysis was the major they called home during that time. Classes for a Graphic Design minor in combination with coding projects lead to a balance of that technical and colorful side. Rivar would go on to design ‘Chromatic Veil Tarot’, a Tarot Deck inspired from scratch art and color theory. Currently, they intern at Best Point Development as both their Tech Assistant and a Site Builder.
