Alkoris Luven is a centaur of the druid grove known as Iborru. It is a place that sees the sun, known to them as ‘Sol’, as a watchful guide. Everyone some day in their life will get markings on their body, showing that they are ready to leave the grove for the betterment of their home. Alkoris and his wife Lilly both get theirs when a poison starts killing the plants in their land. During their journey, Lilly goes missing and Alkoris abandons the mission temporarily to find her
